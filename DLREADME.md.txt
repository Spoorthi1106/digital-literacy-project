 5# 📘 Digital Literacy Project

This project is created as part of a Digital Literacy Portfolio. The aim of this project is to understand and apply digital skills in real-life scenarios. It includes tasks related to infographic creation, digital platforms, coding, email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To develop digital literacy skills  
- To learn safe and responsible use of the internet  
- To explore different digital platforms  
- To understand cyber safety and communication
